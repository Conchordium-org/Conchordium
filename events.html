Our Events

Conchordium hosts various events throughout the school year. Stay tuned for the next event we have for music students!

All Ongoing Upcoming Past

Art of Music Design Contest

What would the world be like without music? The Art of Music Design Contest allows you to explore this question with your imagination and creative arts! Each winner will receive a $25 Curry's gift card.

Narrative Project

The Narrative Project is a great way for youths (ages 12-22) to showcase their talent in music composition through a competition! Finalists will be invited to perform at Conchordium’s “Original Musician’s Benefit Night Concert” at the end of February 2020.

Espress Yourself

A music-oriented coffeehouse where pieces of all genres can be performed. More information coming soon!

Young Leader in Music Award

Congratulations to Vanessa Ng, Matthew Deo, and XiongXiong Pei for their contributions to the community through music!

Transpose: A Music Innovation Conference

Transpose: A Music Innovation Conference, was held on June 9th, 2019. Over 25 individuals from across the GTA gathered at 123 Edward Street to learn about the intersection of music with other industries.
